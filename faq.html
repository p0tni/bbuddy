How to Buy?


To buy bitcoin, you must first sign up for a bitcoin wallet. Traditional payment methods such as a credit card, bank transfer (ACH), or debit cards will buy bitcoins on a bitcoin exchange using a bitcoin wallet as a depository.

How to Trade?


There are various factors that determine what type of bitcoin trading to pursue. Such factors may range from time horizon and risk tolerance to your style of analysis and your expectations of what bitcoin trading may lead to.
